*A city within [[Grand Duchy of Hethlon]]*

Everbrook is the capital of [[Grand Duchy of Hethlon]], it is a bustling trade city in the south western center of Grand Duchy of Hethlon. Everbrook is made up of multiple districts.

1. [[Cloudring]]
2. [[Riverridge]]
3. [[Forrestway]]
4. [[StarCrest District]]
5. [[Ashenknots]]
